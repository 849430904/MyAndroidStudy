– toggleDevice:fromDevices:withCompletion:

A method used to toggle a device given a device dsn transmitted from the WatchKit App. Works for boolean device properties and simply creates a datapoint for the opposite of the current device state. Corresponds to toggleDeviceThroughParentApp:withCurrentStatus:andCompletion: within the WatchKit Extension.

- (void)toggleDevice:(NSString *)dsnToToggle fromDevices:(NSMutableArray *)deviceArray withCompletion:(void ( ^ ) ( NSMutableArray *deviceArray , NSString *errorText ))completionBlock

Parameters

dsnToToggle

A string for the target device’s dsn
deviceArray

An array of known devices, passed from the App Delegate
completionBlock

A block called when the toggle operation has completed. Used to send a reply to the WatchKit App.

Discussion

A method used to toggle a device given a device dsn transmitted from the WatchKit App. Works for boolean device properties and simply creates a datapoint for the opposite of the current device state. Corresponds to toggleDeviceThroughParentApp:withCurrentStatus:andCompletion: within the WatchKit Extension.
