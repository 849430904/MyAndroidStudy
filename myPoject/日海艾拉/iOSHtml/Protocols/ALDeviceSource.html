ALDeviceSource Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSFastEnumeration
NSObject
Declared in ALDeviceSource.h

Overview

Describes an object capable of returning a series of AylaDevice, for instance an ALGroup, or the AylaDeviceManager AMAP category. The class conforming this device should use the AylaDeviceManager linked to the AylaSessionManager as the final source of the devices to keep the instances in sync and avoid having copies of the AylaDevices, the classes of ALDeviceSource could however keep the DSN’s of the devices.
